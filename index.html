Privacy Policy for SnapSpence

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

SnapSpence ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the SnapSpence mobile application.

1. Information Overview

SnapSpence is designed as an offline-first application. Your financial data, including income, obligations, savings, and receipt images, is stored locally on your device.

2. Data Collection and Usage

  • Financial Data: All financial information you enter is stored in your device’s private storage (Core Data) and is used solely to provide financial tracking, insights, and health scores.
  • Receipt Images: Scanned receipt images are stored locally on your device.
  • AI Insights: When you use SnapSpence AI features, relevant financial snapshots are sent securely to Google Gemini APIs for processing. This data is used only for generating real-time insights and is not stored by our servers or used for model training or advertising.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data (such as screen views and button interactions) to improve app performance and user experience. No personally identifiable or financial data is sent to Firebase.

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored locally on your device and may be included in your personal iCloud backup according to standard iOS system settings. We do not have access to your financial data or receipt images.

4. Subscriptions

Payments and subscription management are handled by Apple and RevenueCat. SnapSpence does not collect or store payment details. Please refer to Apple’s and RevenueCat’s respective privacy policies for more information.

5. Your Rights

Because your data is stored locally, you retain full control over it. You may delete individual records or uninstall the app at any time to permanently remove all associated data.
